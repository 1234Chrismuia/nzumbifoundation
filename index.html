OUR FOUNDATION

About Nzumbi Foundation

A family-driven philanthropic organization transforming lives since 2025

Who We Are

Nzumbi Foundation is a family-driven philanthropic organization established to improve livelihoods through structured giving, empowerment, and social welfare programs.

Our Sustainable Funding Model

10% Family Commitment

Consistent monthly funding from 10% of founding family earnings

Community Contributions

Additional support from well-wishers and strategic partners

The Challenge We Address

Communities in Makueni and surrounding counties face:

  • Poverty and youth unemployment
  • Limited access to education
  • Weak household resilience
  • Inadequate social support systems
